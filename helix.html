Helix At a Glimpse

Our Flagship Even-Semester Fest at a Glance


HACK 18

12-hour hackathon where students were given different topics and had to build an application/website on chosen topic. The team with the best app/website was decalared as winner.

Cipher Hunt

Cryptography based online event. A series of questions were posted at regular intervals​ ​and​ ​teams​ ​had​ ​to​ ​solve​ ​the​ ​question​ ​as​ ​quickly​ ​as​ ​possible.

Coder of The Year

Competitive coding event where participants had to solve a few questions. The problems were designed differently for every year. From​ each ​year category,​ ​on​e ​winner​ ​was​ ​declared.

Workshop on GSOC by GDG

Workshop in association with Google Developer's Group, Jalandhar, to tell students about the Google Summer of Code, an open-source development summer programme conducted by Google annually. At the end of the session, official Google Goodies were distributed among the attendees.
