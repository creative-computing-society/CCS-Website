About US

CCS is one of the most elite technical societies of Thapar Institute Of Engineering and Technology. We strive to encourage students to develop an analytical temperament in the field of technology and innovation, nurture creativity and talent and spread awareness about the ever evolving tech world. We act as a platform for students to showcase and improve their skills and in doing so, discover that their best strengths lie within their own self. Over the years, the society has had numerous fruitful collaborations with organisations like Google Developers Group, Women Techmakers, Mozilla Webmaker and PyData, to name a few.

From the President's Desk


Dr. Inderveer Chana, Professor and Associate Head, CSED

  Creative Computing Society is a platform for budding engineers to hone their technical as well as managerial skills creatively. Various workshops and expert talks keep the members conversant with the upcoming technologies that prepares them well for the outside world. Discussion on higher studies and hands on events exposes the undergraduates to new technical streams. Apart from this, the event management skills that the students attain as part of hosting and conducting numerous events round the year imbibes them with good managerial skills. On the whole, the society endeavors to render a wholesome learning environment that not only complements the academics but also the overall development of an undergraduate student at TIET.
